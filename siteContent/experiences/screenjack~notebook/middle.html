The Story

It’s been years since Noah and Allie last saw each other and, you know... people change. What secrets do the couple have that they need to share with the other?

It’s up to you to fill in the dialogue and choose what Noah and Allie say to each other and how the scene plays out.

 

True Love is a word bubble type of Screenjack. While you watch the movie, special word bubbles will pop up on top of the video with your answers and the characters’ dialogue inside!
