The Story

Casey Becker is a teenage babysitter who suddenly gets a phone call from a stranger while home alone. She doesn’t know who this man is or what he wants, but has a few questions he’d like to ask...

It’s up to you to fill in the dialogue and choose what the man on the phone says and how the scene plays out.

 

A Late Night Phonecall Gone Wrong is a text-to-speech type of Screenjack. The caller's original audio track has been removed, and instead we’ve replaced it with a new voice and your answers instead!
