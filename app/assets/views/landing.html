What is Screenjack?

Screenjack is a video-style fill-in-the-blank word game that lets you create hilarity out of featured clips from popular movies and television.

When you start, we provide you a variety of genre-based categories to choose from.

After you select a category, “edit” the video’s script by filling in the blanks based on the prompt provided.

When you’re finished, your custom Screenjack video will be created. All you have to do is sit back, relax, and enjoy the show!
