The Story

It‘s tough when you don‘t get along with your parents. When this kid has had enough, he decides to run away from home. Not to worry, Bumblebee is always there as the voice of reason!

It‘s up to you to fill in the dialogue and choose what Bumblebee says and how the scene plays out.

 

The Runaway is a text-to-speech type of Screenjack. Bumblebee‘s original audio track has been removed, and instead we‘ve replaced it with a new voice and your answers instead!
