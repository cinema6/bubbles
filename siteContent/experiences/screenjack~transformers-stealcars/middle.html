The Story

When some kids decide its time to help themselves to a fancy new sports car, Tracks is here to show them the error of their ways.

It‘s up to you to fill in the dialogue and choose what Tracks says and how the scene plays out.

 

Stealing Cars is a text-to-speech type of Screenjack. Tracks‘ original audio track has been removed, and instead we‘ve replaced it with a new voice and your answers instead!
