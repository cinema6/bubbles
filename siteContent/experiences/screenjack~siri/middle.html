The Story

Meet Siri and control your iPhone Just by talking to her as you would to a person: "Do I need an umbrella?" or "Any great burgers around here?" or "Where's the closest ATM?" Siri not only understands what you say, it knows what you mean. Really, we promise.

It’s up to you to fill in the dialogue and choose what Siri says and how the scene plays out.

 

Get to Know Siri is a text-to-speech type of Screenjack. Siri's original audio track has been removed, and instead we’ve replaced it with a new voice and your answers instead!
